About Us

"Alone we can do so little; together we can do so much"

The problem

No one will deny on the fact that searching for scholarships is one of the most boring and tedious tasks they ever did on the internet! We faced the same problem too. So we built ScholFin which makes this process easy, friendly and fast.

What is ScholFin?

For students: ScholFin is a scholarship search engine which helps students to find perfectly matching scholarships in which they are eligible in just 3 clicks! The total worth of scholarship on our portal is 9,458 crore.

For scholarship providers: ScholFin helps them to create application forms, accept online applications, verify their identity and reward scholarships to the most deserving and needy student.

For companies ScholFin is a unique and most effective platform to spend the CSR money in such a way that it reaches the most deserving and meritorious students who are in need of financial assistance.

Do you know?

According to our research, the total no. of scholarships in India is around 9 crore and the total no. of students according to the Wikipedia is 30 crore. That means that Out of every 3 students in India, One student must get a scholarship. Surprisingly, that’s not true!
Reasons – Unawareness, Corruption and the many pitfalls in our education system!

Our Vision

We want to become the India's best resource to discover opportunities, which you might be unaware of and help you with tough financial conditions. We help you today so you have a brighter tomorrow!
